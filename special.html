The Journey

Every line of code tells a story. This is a look back at how my passion for technology evolved into a career of building scalable digital ecosystems.

2026

Architecting Scale

Graduating with a Bachelor of Computer Applications, I transitioned fully into mastering full-stack ecosystems. By utilizing tools like Next.js, Prisma, and PostgreSQL, I started building applications capable of handling massive user bases with zero latency.

2025

Professional Crucible

My roles at Codnestx and ApnaWebX thrust me into real-world production environments. I learned the critical importance of writing clean, maintainable code, implementing secure JWT authentication, and optimizing complex MongoDB aggregation pipelines for speed.

2023

The Paradigm Shift

Entering SVGU for my BCA degree marked a turning point. I moved beyond static web pages and discovered the power of JavaScript. React.js fundamentally changed how I viewed UI development, while Node.js unlocked the backend. The MERN stack became my canvas.

2020

The First Keystrokes

During my secondary education, a deep curiosity about how websites worked led me to write my very first HTML tags. The thrill of seeing code render on a screen sparked a lifelong obsession with digital creation.
